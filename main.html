Tipparost Thai Restaurant

Thai Style

If you haven’t tried Tipparost Thai Restaurant, you don’t know what you’re missing! Discover authentic Thai cuisine right here in Papakura!

When you visit Tipparost Thai Restaurant you will find a Thai restaurant with all the charm and hospitality that the Thai people are known for! We use the freshest ingredients and traditional methods to prepare all your favourite Thai dishes. Our restaurant is comfortable and relaxed, suitable for special occasions, corporate dinners and lunches or a casual dining experience.

We offer an extensive take away menu available for pick up. Make a booking or order ahead for delicious authentic Thai cuisine on 09 298 6174!

Our restaurant is at 59 O'Shannessey St Auckland 2110 and opens from 5:00 pm to 10:00 pm, Tuesday-Sunday.

We also offer online booking via UBER EAT.
